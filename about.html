Our Story

Beauty, elevated.

GlamEye started in a small Los Angeles studio in 2024 with a question: why does luxury have to mean exclusive — or unkind?

We saw an industry where premium false eyelashes were either over-priced trophies or cheap, throwaway products. We saw an industry where "cruelty-free" was often a marketing word, not a sourcing decision.

So we built a different brand. We hand-design every style in our LA studio. We partner with ethical suppliers who never harm animals. We use packaging you can reuse. And we sell direct, so we can offer truly luxurious lashes at honest prices.

"Luxury isn't a price tag. It's how something is made — and how it makes you feel."

— GlamEye Founder
