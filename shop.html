Quick Answers

Before You Buy

What's the difference between cluster lashes and strip lashes?

Cluster lashes are small individual bundles you apply under your natural lashes (not on top of the lid like a strip). Result: invisible band, more comfortable, customizable density. You build the look in 5–10 minutes, and a single kit gives you 5–7 full applications.

How long do they stay on?

With our BOND adhesive, expect 3–7 days of continuous wear (sleep, shower, gym). The SEAL pen locks the bond and adds waterproof/sweat-proof retention. Remove gently with the REMOVER pen — never pull.

Which kit should I pick first?

If you've never worn cluster lashes, start with Daily Bare or Natural Bloom — they're the most forgiving and look the most like your own lashes. Going to an event? Drama Spike or Fox Eye.

What's your return policy?

30-day returns on unopened kits, no questions asked. Opened items can be returned if defective — just email us at support@glameyeshop.com with a photo.

How long does shipping take?

Orders ship within 1–2 business days from California. U.S. ground delivery is typically 3–5 business days. Free for orders over $50.

Are your lashes cruelty-free?

100%. All GlamEye cluster fibers are synthetic Pro Fibers — fully vegan, no animal products. Our adhesives are latex-free and dermatologist-tested.
