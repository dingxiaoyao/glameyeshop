Quick Answers

Before You Buy

How do I pick between mink, faux mink, and magnetic?

Mink is the most natural-looking and reusable up to 25 times — premium feel, premium price. Faux mink is the vegan, animal-free version with the same look, slightly less wear life. Magnetic needs no glue — easiest to apply if you're new to falsies.

What's your return policy?

30-day returns on unopened items, no questions asked. Opened items can be returned if defective — just email us at support@glameyeshop.com with a photo.

How long does shipping take?

Orders ship within 1–2 business days from Los Angeles. U.S. ground delivery is typically 3–5 business days. Free for orders over $50.

Are your lashes cruelty-free?

100%. Our mink line uses brushed (not pulled) fur, certified by Leaping Bunny. Our faux mink line is fully vegan with synthetic fibers indistinguishable from the real thing.

How many times can I wear them?

Premium mink: up to 25 wears with proper care. Faux mink: 15–20. Magnetic: 30+. Care guide is included with every order.
